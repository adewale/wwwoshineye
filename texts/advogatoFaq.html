The Abode::Advogato Frequently Asked Questions

Advogato Frequently Asked Questions

This will do till someone else gets round to writing a proper Advogato FAQ.

The site's current status
Q: What is the current status of the site?
A: As of the 6th of September 2006 the site was going to be dead and continued on in read-only mode. Raph explained why but there's currently some discussion about keeping the site alive under the stewardship of Steven Rainwater.

XML-RPC
Q: Where can I find information about Advogato's XML-RPC interface?
A: http://www.advogato.org/xmlrpc.html

Q: Where is the "diary" variable declared in the example python code?
A: It isn't declared. To get the examples to work you need to replace all references to "dairy" with "server.diary".

Q: Where is the authenticate method?
A: The authenticate method belongs to the server object. Use server.authenticate(userString, passwordString).

Certifications and the trust metric
Q: Where is the trust metric explained?
A: http://www.advogato.org/trust-metric.html

Q: Where are the certification guidelines?
A: http://www.advogato.org/certs.html

RSS
Q: Does Advogato have an RSS feed? If so, where can I get it?
A: An RSS 0.91 feed of the articles is available at http://www.advogato.org/rss/articles.xml

Q:Can I read particular people's dairies via RSS?
A: Yes. Just type in: http://www.advogato.org/person/THEIR_USERNAME/rss.xml

Q: Can I get the recentlog as an RSS feed?
A: No.

Diary ratings
Q: Why do diaries have numbers rating them? Why doesn't the recentlog show me everybody's diaries?
A: Read the following diary entries by Raph.

  • http://www.advogato.org/person/raph/diary.html?start=223
  • http://www.advogato.org/person/raph/diary.html?start=224
  • http://www.advogato.org/person/raph/diary.html?start=225
  • http://www.advogato.org/person/raph/diary.html?start=280


Q: Why doesn't my diary show up on the recentlog?
A: Chances are your rating is lower than the default threshold for recentlog which is 3. This usually means that those who 'certed' you aren't very fond of your diary entries.

Q: How do I see the values of all the diaries that I have directly and indirectly rated?
A: http://www.advogato.org/rating/report/YOUR_USERNAME

Q: How do I see the values of the diaries that someone else has directly and indirectly rated?
A: http://www.advogato.org/rating/report/THEIR_USERNAME

Q: What do all those figures mean?
A: See http://www.advogato.org/person/raph/diary.html?start=224 for an explanation

Q: How does all this work?
A: See the Trust Metric Howto at http://www.levien.com/free/tmetric-HOWTO.html
